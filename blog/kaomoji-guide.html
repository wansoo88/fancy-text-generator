ğŸ“š Kaomoji History & Culture

Kaomoji originated in Japan in the 1980s on early internet forums and bulletin board systems. Unlike Western emoticons (like :) or :D), kaomoji are designed to be read without tilting your head.

  • 1986: First kaomoji appeared on Japanese forums
  • 1990s: Spread through Japanese mobile phones
  • 2000s: Became popular worldwide through anime and gaming communities
  • Today: Used globally on social media and messaging apps
