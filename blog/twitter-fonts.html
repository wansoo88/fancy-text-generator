Tips for Using Fonts on Twitter

Frequently Asked Questions

Will fancy fonts affect my Twitter SEO?

Unicode characters may not be indexed the same way as regular text. For important keywords, consider using regular text alongside fancy fonts.

Do fancy fonts work on Twitter Blue/X Premium?

Yes! Fancy fonts work the same way regardless of your subscription status.

Can I use fancy fonts in Twitter ads?

While technically possible, Twitter's ad policies may restrict certain characters. Test your ads before launching.
