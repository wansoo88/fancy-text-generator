What Are Aesthetic Fonts?

Aesthetic fonts aren't actually "fonts" in the traditional sense. They're Unicode characters that look like different font styles. Because they're real characters (not custom fonts), they work everywhere - Instagram, Twitter, TikTok, Discord, and more!

Common Questions

Why don't aesthetic fonts work on some platforms?

Some platforms filter out certain Unicode characters. Also, older devices may not have fonts installed that support all Unicode characters.

Are aesthetic fonts safe to use?

Yes! They're just regular Unicode characters. However, some platforms may have rules against certain characters in usernames.
